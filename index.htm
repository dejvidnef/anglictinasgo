Intermediate Test Bank MultiROM

Introduction

Introduction

Welcome to the insight Intermediate Test Bank MultiROM – the complete teacher resource for continuous assessment, with materials that you can customize for your students and your teaching situation.

This MultiROM works in two ways:

In your computer it has tests as PDFs and Word documents, answer keys and audio scripts.
In your CD player you can play the audio tracks for the listening exercises.

The tests on this MultiROM

All the tests are provided as PDFs and as Word documents. You can print out the PDFs and use them as they are. (If you don't have Adobe Reader, get it from the Adobe website.) If you want to adapt the tests, you can edit the Word documents and save them on your computer. If you have any problems opening the documents, go to the documents folder on the MultiROM. There are two versions (A and B) of the Grammar and Vocabulary Tests, the Skills Tests and the Cumulative Review Tests. The Listening and Reading sections contain the same reading texts and audio in each version, but the questions are different. The Vocabulary, Grammar, Everyday English, Writing and Challenge! sections are slightly different in each version. If your students don't copy each other, then you can just use either test. If your students do copy each other, then put them in pairs, one with an A test and one with a B test. All the tests can be photocopied freely for classroom use. They may not be sold without the permission of Oxford University Press.

Guidelines for adapting the tests

You might want to add something extra you taught in class, remove something you decided to omit or didn't have time to teach, or even personalize the exercises. You can easily do this by:

  • adding, removing or editing sections.
  • adding, removing or editing questions.

You might also want to:

  • put two tests together – for instance if you want a quick Vocabulary and Grammar test for Units 1 and 2, you could combine these tests for Units 1 and 2.
  • make tests which only cover Vocabulary or Grammar – you might want to give your students a test on the Grammar from Units 1, 2 and 3, so you could combine the Grammar sections from these units.
  • make the layout compatible with other tests you use.
  • use the audio scripts provided and write extra comprehension questions, or vocabulary or pronunciation exercises.

Remember to save your customized tests on your computer if you want to use them again.

Vocabulary and Grammar Tests for each unit of the book – A and B versions

20 minutes

There is one test per unit. These comprehensive tests cover Grammar and Vocabulary for each unit of the insight Intermediate Student’s Book. Each test is provided as two versions (A and B), in both Word and PDF formats.

Skills Tests for each unit of the book – A and B versions

40 minutes

There is one test per unit. These comprehensive tests cover Everyday English, Listening, Reading and Writing for each unit of the insight Intermediate Student’s Book. Each test also includes an optional Challenge! activity that students can do at home or in another lesson. The Challenge! activity has been designed to review both the grammar and vocabulary that has been taught in previous units. Each test is provided as two versions (A and B), in both Word and PDF formats.

Cumulative Tests (Units 1–5 and Units 1–10) – A and B versions

20 minutes Vocabulary and Grammar, 40 minutes Skills

These Cumulative Tests provide the opportunity to test the first half of insight Intermediate, as well as all ten units at the end of the year. They follow the same format as the unit Vocabulary and Grammar, and Skills Tests. Each Cumulative Skills Test also includes an optional speaking activity. Each test is provided as two versions (A and B) in both Word and PDF formats.

Audio

The Skills Tests and the Cumulative Skills Tests include listening exercises. The scripts for this material are included in the 'Answer Keys & Audio Scripts' and can be found in the main menu. The audio tracks are on this disk, and will play in a normal CD player.
